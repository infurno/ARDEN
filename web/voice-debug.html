Troubleshooting Tips

  • No microphone access? Check browser permissions (lock icon in address bar)
  • Recording too short? Hold button for at least 1 second while speaking
  • No audio detected? Speak louder or check microphone hardware
  • Button disabled? Browser doesn't support audio recording
  • Still not working? Try refreshing the page and re-allowing permissions

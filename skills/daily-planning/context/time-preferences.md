# Time Preferences

## Overview
Your personal scheduling preferences and energy patterns for optimal time blocking.

## Working Hours

### Core Hours
- **Start**: 8:00 AM
- **End**: 5:00 PM
- **Total**: 9 hours

### Protected Time
These times are protected from meetings:
- 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM: Morning routine & planning
- 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM: Lunch (no meetings)
- 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM: Wrap up & review

### Availability for Meetings
- **Best times**: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM, 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM
- **Acceptable**: 9:00 AM - 10:00 AM
- **Avoid**: 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM, 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM, 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM

## Energy Patterns

### Chronotype: Morning Person

**High Energy (Peak Performance)**
- Time: 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM
- Best for: Deep work, complex problem solving, creative tasks
- Protect fiercely: No meetings, no interruptions

**Medium Energy (Good for Collaboration)**
- Time: 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM
- Best for: Meetings, collaborative work, reviews
- Acceptable: Some interruptions, team activities

**Low Energy (Maintenance Mode)**
- Time: 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM
- Best for: Email, admin tasks, organization
- Avoid: Major decisions, complex work

## Time Blocking Defaults

### Block Sizes
- **Deep work**: 90-120 minutes
- **Shallow work**: 30-60 minutes
- **Meetings**: Duration + 15 min buffer
- **Breaks**: 5-15 minutes every 60-90 minutes
- **Lunch**: 60 minutes (protected)

### Buffer Rules
- Before meetings: 15 minutes (prep time)
- After meetings: 15 minutes (notes/follow-up)
- Between deep work blocks: 30 minutes
- End of day: 30 minutes (review)

### Transition Times
- Morning startup: 30 minutes (coffee, email triage)
- After lunch: 15 minutes (get back in flow)
- Meeting transitions: 5-10 minutes
- Day shutdown: 30 minutes (review tomorrow)

## Scheduling Rules

### Hard Rules (Never Break)
1. No meetings before 9:00 AM
2. Lunch is sacred - no meetings 12-1 PM
3. At least 2 hours of deep work daily
4. End day by 5:00 PM for family time

### Soft Rules (Break When Necessary)
1. Prefer mornings for deep work
2. Batch meetings in afternoons
3. Take breaks every 90 minutes
4. Don't schedule back-to-back meetings

### Ideal Day Structure
```
08:00-08:30  Morning routine & coffee
08:30-09:00  Email triage (process, don't act)
09:00-09:30  Daily planning & priority review

09:30-11:00  🎯 DEEP WORK BLOCK 1 (High energy)
11:00-11:15  ☕ Break (step away from screen)
11:15-12:00  Shallow work / Quick tasks

12:00-01:00  🍽️ LUNCH (away from desk)

01:00-03:00  🤝 MEETINGS / COLLABORATION
03:00-03:15  ☕ Break

03:15-04:30  📝 ADMIN / EMAIL / WRAP UP
04:30-05:00  🌅 Evening review & tomorrow plan
```

## Break Preferences

### Frequency
- Take a break every 60-90 minutes of focused work
- Use Pomodoro: 25 min work / 5 min break (optional)

### Break Activities
- [x] Short walk
- [x] Hydration (drink water)
- [x] Stretch / movement
- [x] Look away from screen (20-20-20 rule)
- [x] Deep breathing (1 minute)

### Lunch Ritual
- Duration: 60 minutes
- Location: Away from desk
- Activities: Eat, walk, read, decompress
- No work during lunch

## Seasonal Adjustments

### Winter (Nov-Feb)
- Start slightly later (8:30 AM) - harder to wake up
- More breaks - energy dips more
- End on time - less daylight

### Summer (Jun-Aug)
- Can start earlier (7:30 AM) - more energy
- Longer lunch - enjoy weather
- Lighter schedule on Fridays

## Weekend Boundaries

### Work on Weekends
- Generally: No scheduled work
- Exceptions: True emergencies only
- Occasional: Sunday evening planning (30 min max)

### Digital Detox
- No email checking after 6 PM
- No work apps on phone after hours
- Phone in other room during family time

## Flexibility Clauses

### When to Break Rules
1. True emergencies (customer down, deadline crisis)
2. High-value opportunities (can't reschedule)
3. Team needs (occasional support)
4. Personal peaks (when energy is unusually high/low)

### Recovery Protocol
After breaking schedule rules:
- [ ] Get back on track next day
- [ ] Don't break rules two days in a row
- [ ] Review what caused the exception
- [ ] Adjust future planning if needed

## Time Zone Considerations

### Primary Time Zone
- Local time: Central Time (CT)
- Work hours in UTC: 14:00 - 23:00 UTC

### Cross-Timezone Meetings
- Prefer: 9 AM - 11 AM CT for Asia calls
- Prefer: 2 PM - 4 PM CT for Europe calls
- Avoid: Before 7 AM CT or after 6 PM CT

## Productivity Hacks

### Environment Setup
- Desk: Clean at end of each day
- Lighting: Natural light when possible
- Noise: Quiet for deep work, music for admin
- Notifications: Off during deep work

### Focus Enablers
- Noise-canceling headphones
- Phone in another room
- Browser blockers (social media)
- "Do not disturb" status

### Accountability
- Share schedule with team
- Block calendar as "Focus time"
- Use productivity buddy system
- Track adherence weekly

## Notes
- Preferences may evolve - review quarterly
- Listen to your body and adjust
- Productivity is personal - do what works for you
- Consistency matters more than perfection
